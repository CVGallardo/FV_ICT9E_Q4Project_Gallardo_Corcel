Junia Jónsdóttir

Junia

Júnía Lín Jónsdóttir (Also known as Junia) is a renowned creative director and social media influencer known for her content creation and Instagram presence, she is most notable for being the twin sister of Laufey, the jazz singer who gained fame as a finalist on the TV show, Iceland's Got Talent.


From as early as seven years old, she felt a strong connection to the violin, the same instrument her mother played at the Central Conservatory of Music in Beijing. Her Instagram account, known as junialin, has amassed a following of more than 300k
